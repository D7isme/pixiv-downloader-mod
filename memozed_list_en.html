・How to Use

When you register a PixivID and a string in this list, the ?memoized-name? tag will be replaced with the registered text when downloading illustrations from that author.

・How to Confirm PixivID

To register, visit the user's homepage and input the 01234567 part from the URL https://www.pixiv.net/users/01234567 into the PixivID field.
